About

Mallard Works

Mallard Works is a Bay Area-based enthusiast restoration company focused on producing high-quality components for rare Mazda bumper systems and restoration projects.

Built by Enthusiasts

Mallard Works was created from a passion for preserving rare and difficult-to-source Mazda components that are often no longer available through traditional channels.

Every product is developed with a focus on accurate fitment, clean installation, and long-term durability for enthusiast-owned vehicles.

Low-Volume, Boutique Manufacturing

Products are manufactured in small batches with an emphasis on craftsmanship, quality control, and functional design rather than mass production.

Many components are developed from original OEM parts and refined through CAD modeling, prototyping, and real-world test fitting.

Designed and Manufactured in the USA

Mallard Works products are designed and manufactured in the United States with a focus on supporting the long-term preservation of rare enthusiast vehicles and restoration communities.
