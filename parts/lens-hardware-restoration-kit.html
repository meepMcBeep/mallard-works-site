Restoration Hardware

Lens Hardware Restoration Kit

Replacement lens hardware kit designed to restore bumper lens mounting hardware for rare Mazda bumper lenses where original clips are missing, damaged, or difficult to source.

Details

  • Designed to support lens hardware restoration
  • Developed for Mazdaspeed A-Spec bumper applications
  • Designed and manufactured in the USA
  • Developed from original lens mounting clip geometry for precise fitment
  • CAD-designed and test-fit for accurate installation

Kit Contents

  • Two replacement lens mounting clips
  • Two m3 x 5mm flat head screws
  • Two m3 washers
  • Two m3 well nuts
  • Two m3 x 15mm flat head screws
  • Four m4 speed nuts
  • Four m4 button head flange screws
  • Thin foam weatherstripping

Notes

  • Lenses not included
